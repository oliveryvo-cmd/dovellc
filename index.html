Our Mission

Why we exist

D.O.V.E LLC was founded by Oliver Vo, a 10-year-old entrepreneur with Tourette syndrome, on one simple belief — disability is not a weakness. It is a superpower.

We are a platform for young entrepreneurs, creators, and builders who are disabled, different, or just built different. We build games, companies, products, and movements. We don't ask for permission. We build.

💪

Empowerment

We turn what makes you different into what makes you powerful.

🛠️

Build

Every member is a creator. Games, companies, art — we make things.

🤝

Community

No negativity. No bullying. Just builders supporting builders.

🌍

Impact

We want to change what the world thinks disability means.
